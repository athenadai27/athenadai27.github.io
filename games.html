Pinrut.

The Process.

I directed a team of seven individuals in creating what we dubbed a "2D Boss Rush Platformer". Wanting to play with ideas of the platforms controlling the actions of the player and trying to pit the environment against the player, we ended with a colorful, bombastic, quaint little platformer that tests users in a variety of new platforming challenges every level. No level is the same, from art to mechanics to music.

Special thanks to: Alan Guan, Alex Ma, Alex Vuong, Bryant Ta, Sijay Liu, Stacy Li, and Vanessa Wang for their work, and Joshua Baysa for lending his voice.

Created in Unity Engine.

High School Simulator.

The Process.

Over the summer of '19, I met individuals at my job as a server from varieties and backgrounds I had never met before. In that same summer, I grew frustrated talking with a friend as she mentioned college was the only path for individuals to take, that those who didn't go to college wouldn't get anywhere in life, and "c'mon, all you need is an application and you're done".

I wanted to capture the variety of experiences an individual can have at high school. Not everyone is a one-track brain surgeon, and that's alright. In this game, you'll lead a randomly generated high schooler, random from their stats, to their goals, even their appearance. I want to capture our experiences at high school; how one ten-question vocab quiz was still life or death, the chilling fear of missing the bus and needing to wake up your mom, the panic of thinking that four years decided the rest of your life.

*this game is still pre-alpha; links will be updated as more progress is made. :)
