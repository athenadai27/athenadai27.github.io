Hi there! My name is Athena, and I'm currently an aspiring game designer studying computer science at UCLA.

I'm a believer in pushing my limits; if I can draw with pencil and paper, then I can try paint, digital, or animation. If I can play piano, then I can learn to write my own sheet music, or mix my own audio. If I can love playing video games, then I can make them too.

I believe video games are trying to push the limits of art and technology as well; and I wish to be there with the medium every step of the way.
